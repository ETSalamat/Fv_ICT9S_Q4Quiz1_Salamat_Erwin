    • CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE
    Characters Description
    Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin
    • Ibarra is a young and successful Filipino who is well-known for his seven year education outside the country in Europe.
    • He is generally admired by many in the country, smart, and an idealistic man.
    • He is the love of Maria Clara whom he left a letter for before he went to study in Europe.
    Padre Damaso
    • Padre Damaso is a priest who is known to be corrupt, ignorant, and doesn’t care about Indios.
    • He has lived in the Philippines for more than twenty years but he barely knows how to speak Tagalog.
    • He also has a bad relationship with Ibarra due to a past conflict.
    Maria Clara
    • Maria is a respected, obedient, and loving woman.
    • It was believed that she was the child of Kapitan Tiago at first but Padre Damaso is her real biological father.
    • She is Ibarra’s love and wants to get married with him but Padre Damaso's interferance with their relationship is affecting it.
